## About Me

I am an Information Technology student at PTIT with a strong interest in **AI Agents, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), Multi-Agent Systems, and practical LLM applications**.

My focus is not only on learning AI concepts, but also on turning them into usable systems: agents that can use tools, retrieve reliable information, follow workflows, and support human decision-making safely.

At the moment, I am learning by building small but practical AI projects with **Python, LangGraph, LangChain, CrewAI, vector databases, and Docker**.

---

## Current Focus

- Building AI agent workflows with **LangGraph** and **CrewAI**
- Exploring **RAG pipelines** with chunking, retrieval, reranking, and evaluation
- Learning how to connect LLMs with tools, APIs, local files, and databases
- Practicing **Human-in-the-Loop** validation for safer AI behavior
- Improving my foundation in **Machine Learning**, **Deep Learning**, and software engineering
